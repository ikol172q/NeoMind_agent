# Ethereum Dollar-Cost Averaging

## What it is
Same mechanic as Bitcoin DCA, applied to Ether (ETH). Buy a fixed dollar
amount on a regular schedule. Optionally stake ETH for additional yield
(~3-5% APR) — staking carries different tax treatment and complexity.
Self-custody for long-term holds; consider hardware wallet support for ETH.

## When it works
- **Crypto bull markets**: ETH typically outperforms BTC in altcoin-led
  rallies (2017, 2021).
- **Long-duration capital**: 4+ year horizons.
- **Behavioral protection** vs trying to time entries.

## When it fails
- **Drawdowns deeper than BTC**: 2022 saw ETH drop ~80%+ from peak. ETH/BTC
  ratio also declines in bear markets, hurting ETH more than BTC.
- **Smart-contract / protocol risk**: ETH's value depends on the network's
  continued utility; new L1 competition (Solana) is real.
- **Staking complexity**: liquid staking vs solo staking, slashing risk on
  validator misbehavior, illiquidity periods.
- **Custody risk** as with all crypto.

## Tax & compliance considerations
- Property treatment per IRS Notice 2014-21.
- No wash-sale rule (as of early 2026 — verify current law).
- Hold >12 months for LTCG.
- **Staking rewards** are ordinary income at the FAIR MARKET VALUE on the
  date received (Rev. Rul. 2023-14). Each staking distribution creates
  ordinary income; cost basis for that ETH is the FMV recognized.
- Subsequent sale of staked ETH triggers another taxable event.

## $10k feasibility
Trivial. Coinbase / Kraken / Fidelity allow fractional purchases. Allocate
1-5% of $10k as a satellite ($100-500).

## First-week starter checklist
1. Pick a venue (Coinbase, Kraken, Fidelity Crypto). Same one as BTC for
   simplicity.
2. Set automatic recurring buy: $25-50/week ETH.
3. **Decide upfront** whether to stake. If yes:
   - Liquid staking via Lido (stETH) is simpler but has slight depeg risk.
   - Native staking via Coinbase requires lockup.
   - Solo staking requires 32 ETH and technical setup.
4. Track staking rewards separately for tax — each reward is taxable income.
5. Self-custody once balance is meaningful.
