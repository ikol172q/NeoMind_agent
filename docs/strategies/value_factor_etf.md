# Value Factor ETF (VTV / VLUE / RPV)

## What it is
A value-factor ETF systematically holds stocks that are cheap on
fundamentals — typically low price-to-earnings, low price-to-book, low
price-to-cash-flow. Fama and French documented the value premium in 1992 as
one of the first systematic factors beyond market beta. Common implementations:
Vanguard VTV (large-cap value), iShares VLUE (multi-factor MSCI value), and
Invesco RPV (deeper-value, S&P 500 Pure Value).

## When it works
- **Inflation / rate-rising regimes**: 2022 was a banner year for value as
  growth multiples compressed.
- **Mean-reversion phases after growth bubbles**: 2000-2007 was a value
  decade post-dot-com.
- **Globally documented premium**: ~2-4%/yr historical in most developed
  markets (Fama-French data).

## When it fails
- **Decade-long underperformance is normal**: 2010-2020 value lost to growth
  by ~3-4%/yr. Investors who quit during that period missed the 2022-2023
  reversal.
- **Value traps**: cheap-on-paper stocks may be permanently impaired (Sears,
  GE peak years, traditional media).
- **Factor crowding**: post-2017 academic and ETF flows arguably eroded the
  premium.
- **Definitional drift**: VTV (price/book) vs VLUE (multi-factor) deliver
  different exposures.

## Tax & compliance considerations
- ETF wrapper: standard tax treatment. Hold >12 months for LTCG.
- Most distributions are qualified dividends → LTCG rates if holding-period
  rule met (IRS Pub 550).
- Wash-sale risk low for buy-and-hold; rises if tax-loss harvesting between
  similar value ETFs (VTV ↔ IVE may be substantially identical — IRS hasn't
  ruled definitively).

## $10k feasibility
Trivial. $500-2,000 satellite allocation works at any size. Fractional shares
available.

## First-week starter checklist
1. Pick one ETF: VTV (cheap, large-cap), VLUE (multi-factor), or RPV (deep
   value, smaller).
2. Buy $1,000-2,000 as a tilt alongside core index (VTI).
3. Commit to 5+ year evaluation horizon — value's underperformance windows
   are long.
4. Avoid swapping between similar value ETFs at a loss within 30 days
   without a tax-pro check.
