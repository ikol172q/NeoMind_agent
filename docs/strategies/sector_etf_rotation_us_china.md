# US/China Sector Rotation (SOXX / KWEB / MCHI)

## What it is
A three-way relative-strength rotation across SOXX (US semiconductors), KWEB
(China internet), and MCHI (broad China). Each captures a different macro
narrative — US tech / Chinese stimulus / China broad equity. Strategy: every
week, compute the trailing 3-month total return for each ETF; hold the top 2,
50/50 weight. Rebalance only when the leaderboard changes by at least one
slot. This is a tactical version that exploits divergence between US-tech and
China-tech regimes.

## When it works
- **Clear regime divergence**: 2023 H1 saw KWEB rally on China reopening
  while SOXX rallied on AI — both outperformed broad SPY.
- **Policy-driven moves**: Chinese stimulus announcements catalyze KWEB/MCHI;
  AI-cycle catalysts move SOXX.
- **Diversification of Eastern + Western tech narratives**.

## When it fails
- **Single-headline whipsaws**: July 2021 platform crackdown halved KWEB
  overnight. Trailing 3-month signal is too slow.
- **Correlation collapse**: 2022 saw both SOXX AND China collapse together
  — no place to rotate to.
- **PDT-relevant** if you rotate weekly — at $10k, prefer monthly cadence.
- **ADR-delisting risk**: China ETF holdings face HFCAA risk, partially
  mitigated post-2022 PCAOB deal.

## Tax & compliance considerations
- Rotations held 1-3 months → short-term capital gains in most cases.
- KWEB and MCHI distribute foreign-source dividends; foreign tax credit
  available via Form 1116 (best in taxable account, not IRA).
- Wash-sale risk: rotating SOXX → SMH at a loss may be substantially
  identical (both track the same underlying semiconductor index family).
- IRS Pub 550 covers FTC and wash-sale rules.

## $10k feasibility
Feasible. Two 50/50 positions of ~$5k each. Monthly rebalance cadence keeps
PDT count well under threshold (estimated 4-8 round trips/year).

## First-week starter checklist
1. Build a tracking sheet for SOXX, KWEB, MCHI 3-month total returns.
2. Decide rebalance cadence — recommend monthly for $10k investors.
3. Initial allocation: top 2 of the 3 ETFs, 50/50.
4. Set monthly review reminder. Rebalance only on leaderboard change.
5. Track foreign-tax-paid line on year-end 1099-DIV for Form 1116.
