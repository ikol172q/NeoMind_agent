# KWEB Momentum (China Internet ETF)

## What it is
KraneShares CSI China Internet ETF (KWEB) holds the major Chinese internet
platforms — Tencent, Alibaba, Meituan, JD, PDD, NetEase, and others. KWEB
is highly volatile and policy-sensitive. The "momentum" play: enter only
when both the 50-day moving average is above the 200-day moving average AND
the trailing 60-day return is positive. Exit on a violation of either rule.
This is a regime-filtered tactical position, not a buy-and-hold.

## When it works
- **China stimulus / reopening cycles**: 2023 H1 saw KWEB rally 25%+ on
  reopening optimism.
- **Earnings cycles** when sector profitability surprises.
- **USD weakness** can be a tailwind for emerging-market allocations.

## When it fails
- **Single-headline events**: July 2021 platform crackdown (DiDi, edtech)
  halved KWEB. Trend filters were too slow.
- **VIE structure risk**: Chinese internet platforms operate via Variable
  Interest Entities, not direct equity ownership. Legal interpretation is
  Chinese-court-dependent.
- **HFCAA delisting risk**: partly mitigated by 2022 PCAOB-China audit
  agreement, but policy can shift again.
- **Long lost decade for Chinese equities** 2008-2024.

## Tax & compliance considerations
- KWEB holds ADRs and Hong Kong-listed shares. Distributions include
  foreign-source income; **foreign tax credit** available via Form 1116.
  Best held in TAXABLE account (FTC unusable in IRA).
- Hold >12 months for LTCG.
- Most distributions are qualified dividends if holding-period rule met
  (IRS Pub 550).
- Wash-sale risk: no obvious "substantially identical" alternative — KWEB
  vs MCHI hold quite different baskets.

## $10k feasibility
Feasible. Allocate 10-20% of $10k as a tactical satellite. Single ticker;
fractional shares supported.

## First-week starter checklist
1. Compute KWEB 50-DMA and 200-DMA. Confirm 50 > 200 AND trailing 60-day
   return > 0 BEFORE entering.
2. If conditions met, allocate 10-20% of $10k ($1,000-2,000).
3. Set weekly review reminder: re-check the two conditions.
4. Exit immediately if either condition violates. Do NOT average down.
5. Track foreign tax paid for Form 1116 at year-end.
