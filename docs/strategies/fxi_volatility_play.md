# FXI Defined-Risk Volatility Play

## What it is
iShares China Large-Cap ETF (FXI) tracks the FTSE China 50 — a concentrated
basket of the 50 largest Chinese companies traded in Hong Kong. FXI is more
"old economy" weighted than KWEB (financials, energy, utilities heavier).
Volatility around China policy announcements (Politburo meetings, NPC, PBOC
moves) creates trading opportunities. Per user constraint, this catalog
uses ONLY defined-risk option spreads — never naked options.

## When it works
- **High IV-rank moments** (around major China policy events) → richer
  premium for credit spreads, or defined-risk debit spreads to express
  directional view.
- **Policy stimulus announcements** historically catalyze multi-day FXI
  rallies (China bazooka stimulus October 2024).
- **Pre-NPC / Two Sessions setups** (annual March policy meetings).

## When it fails
- **Wide bid-ask** on FXI options vs SPY — slippage matters at $10k.
- **Headline whipsaws**: rumor cycles produce IV crush mid-trade.
- **Stay defined-risk**: FXI's 10%+ overnight gap potential makes naked
  options especially dangerous (which the user prohibits anyway).

## Tax & compliance considerations
- FXI is an equity-option underlying — short-term capital gains/losses
  (NOT §1256).
- Foreign-tax-credit applies only to the FTI ETF's distributions, not to
  options P&L.
- IRS Pub 550 (options taxation, wash-sale).
- Wash-sale risk if you re-establish similar spreads within 30 days at a
  loss.

## $10k feasibility
Feasible. FXI trades around $30, making spreads cheap: a $1-2 wide credit
spread costs $50-150 in buying power. $10k can run multiple concurrent
spreads.

## First-week starter checklist
1. Track FXI IV rank weekly. When IV rank > 50 (typically around major
   China policy events), opportunity is best.
2. To express bullish view: sell a 30-DTE put spread at delta ~0.25, buy
   a put 1-2 strikes lower.
3. To express bearish view: sell a 30-DTE call spread at delta ~0.25, buy
   a call 1-2 strikes higher.
4. Risk per setup ≤ 2% of account ($200 at $10k).
5. Plan: close at 50% of max profit. Defend by rolling at 21 DTE if tested.
