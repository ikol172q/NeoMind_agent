# Bitcoin Dollar-Cost Averaging

## What it is
Apply the same DCA discipline used for index ETFs to Bitcoin: buy a fixed
dollar amount on a regular schedule (weekly or biweekly), regardless of
price. Hold long-term in self-custody (hardware wallet) once the position
exceeds a threshold (e.g., $5,000+). The thesis: BTC is a long-duration
monetary asset whose adoption curve plays out over decades; DCA dampens the
extreme volatility while maintaining exposure.

## When it works
- **Long-term adoption growth**: 2014-2024 BTC compounded at >40%/yr CAGR
  despite multiple 70-85% drawdowns.
- **Behavioral protection**: removes the temptation to time entries
  (typically a losing game with BTC).
- **Volatility-tolerant capital**: capital you won't need for 5+ years.

## When it fails
- **Severe drawdowns are normal**: 80% drops have happened multiple times
  (2014, 2018, 2022). Many DCA-ers stop contributing exactly at the bottom.
- **Custody risk**: holding on exchange exposes to FTX-style failures.
  Self-custody requires technical care.
- **Regulatory shocks**: country-level bans (China 2021), tax-treatment
  reclassifications.
- **Opportunity cost**: capital tied up in BTC is capital not earning
  T-bill yield.

## Tax & compliance considerations
- IRS classifies crypto as **property**, not currency (Notice 2014-21). Each
  sale, swap, or spend is a taxable event.
- **Wash-sale rule does NOT currently apply to crypto** (as of early 2026).
  Multiple legislative proposals would change this — VERIFY current law
  before relying on tax-loss harvesting.
- Hold >12 months for LTCG (0%/15%/20%).
- Track basis carefully — broker 1099-B reporting for crypto is improving
  but historically inconsistent. Use Koinly, CoinTracker, or similar.

## $10k feasibility
Trivial. Coinbase, Kraken, Fidelity Crypto, Robinhood Crypto all support
$10+ purchases. Allocate 1-10% of $10k as a satellite ($100-1,000); avoid
heavy concentration given volatility.

## First-week starter checklist
1. Open a Coinbase, Kraken, or Fidelity Crypto account. KYC verification
   required.
2. Set up automatic recurring buys: $50/week BTC.
3. If balance grows past $5,000, transfer to a hardware wallet (Ledger,
   Trezor) for self-custody. Test with a small amount first.
4. Track every transaction for tax purposes — even crypto-to-crypto swaps
   are taxable.
5. Plan to hold ≥4 years; ride through one full halving cycle.
